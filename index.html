What does this mean?

If your spare cash can earn more than the break-even rate elsewhere, the installment plan wins — you keep money working longer. If it can earn less, paying the lump sum is cheaper. The break-even is the annualized return rate at which the two paths have identical present value.

XIRR — Annualized Return of Irregular Cash Flows

Negative = money out (investment / cost). Positive = money in (proceeds / income). Need at least one of each.

What does this mean?

XIRR is the constant annualized rate at which the present value of all your cash flows sums to zero. It is Excel's XIRR — the standard way to measure return on an irregular series like dividend reinvestment, real-estate ownership, or staged investments.
